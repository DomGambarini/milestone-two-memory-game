    • HOW TO PLAY
    • Choose two cards to click on.
    • Matching cards earn you 7 points! Equivalent to scoring a try in rugby.
    • Mismatched cards is a penalty against you and will minus 3 points from your score.
    • You must unflip every card to complete the game.
    • A minimum of one point is needed to win.
    • Scoring minus one point or less results in a loss.
    • A score of 0 points leads to a draw.
